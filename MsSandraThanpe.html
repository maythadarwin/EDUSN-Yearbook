Dear Students,

          Welcome to our 2023-24 Online Yearbook This Yearbook is a snapshot of the highlights and memorable events of the year, and a record of the students and staff who formed School community in 2021. I hope it will remind you of good times with friends, the new people you met, our diverse cultures, performing PBL presentations your year of learning and striving to be better in all that you do. As the academic year comes to a close, I reflect on the remarkable journey we've shared together. As your Director of Studies, it's been a privilege to witness your growth, passion, and resilience. Each day, you walked through our school's doors with determination and enthusiasm for learning. You embraced challenges with a spirit of curiosity, and your dedication to excellence has been truly inspiring. Our classrooms were transformed into vibrant spaces of creativity and discovery, thanks to your active engagement and eagerness to explore new ideas. Beyond academics, I am proud of the kind-heartedness you have shown towards one another. Your acts of empathy and support have fostered a strong sense of community within our school, making it a place where everyone feels valued and appreciated. As you move on to the next phase of your lives, I encourage you to carry the lessons learned here with you. Embrace challenges as opportunities for growth, maintain a hunger for knowledge, and always approach life with an open heart. Remember, success is not merely defined by achievements, but by the positive impact you make on the lives of others. Strive to be compassionate leaders who uplift and empower those around you. I have no doubt that each one of you will go on to achieve great things, and I eagerly await the news of your accomplishments. Stay true to yourselves, be courageous in pursuing your dreams, and never stop seeking excellence in all that you do. To all who are committed to the EDUSN international Digital School community, and who have made 2023-24 such a successful year, thank you. To you all I extend my very best wishes for the future.



Ms. Sandra T. Pe
Director of Studies
EDUSN international digital school
